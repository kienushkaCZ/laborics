Cookie Policy

Effective date: February 1, 2026 · Last updated: February 1, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Reload dev s.r.o. ("we", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on the Laborics website and platform ("Service"). It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use the service. Some cookies are essential for the site to function; others help us improve your experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies Required

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function. They cannot be disabled. They handle authentication, security, and basic functionality.

Cookie Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your login session Session / 30 days
csrf_token Protects against cross-site request forgery Session
cookie_consent Stores your cookie preferences 12 months

Functional Cookies Optional

These cookies remember your preferences (such as language selection) to provide a more personalized experience.

Cookie Purpose Duration
lang Remembers your language preference 12 months
theme Remembers your display preferences 12 months

Analytics Cookies Optional

These cookies help us understand how visitors use the Service so we can improve it. All data is anonymized. These cookies are only set if you give consent.

Cookie Provider Purpose Duration
_ga, _ga_* Google Analytics Distinguishes visitors, tracks page views and traffic sources Up to 26 months

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services we use:

  • Stripe — may set cookies for fraud prevention and payment processing when you interact with the checkout. See Stripe's Cookie Policy.
  • Google Analytics — used for anonymized traffic analysis (only with your consent). See Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

When you first visit the Service, a cookie consent banner lets you choose which optional cookies to accept. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of the site.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Blocking essential cookies may prevent you from using the Service.

5. Do Not Track

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. We respect DNT signals — if your browser sends one, we will not load optional analytics cookies regardless of your consent banner choice.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when we add or remove cookies, or when regulations change. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
